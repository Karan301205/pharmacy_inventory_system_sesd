# Pharmacy Inventory & Prescription Logger

## Scope
A specialized system for pharmacists to manage medicinal stock, track expiry batches, and maintain logs of dispensed medications to patients.

## Key Features
- **Stock Management:** Add, update, and track medicine batches with expiry dates.
- **Dispense Tracking:** Record every transaction of medicine given to a patient.
- **Expiry Alerts:** Automated marking of "Expired" or "Near Expiry" items.
- **Role-Based Security:** Admin (manage stock) and Pharmacist (dispense only).
